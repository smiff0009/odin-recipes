Fajitas Recipe

Description

Fajitas are a simple but tasty dish you can make any day of the week

Ingredients

  • top sirloin steak
  • bell peppers
  • red onion
  • tortillas
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Cut steak and veggies into slides, lengthwise
  2. Saute onions and peppers over medium heat for a few minutes
  3. Cook steak for 12 minutes
  4. Assemble on tortillas and serve hot
