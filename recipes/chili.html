Chili Recipe

Description

Chili can be made in a lot of different ways, but in this recipe we focus on health by using lean ground turkey meat instead of beef.

Feel free to add more ingredients if you like, but we think this is a great start!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 99% lean ground turkey
  • tomato paste
  • fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 large red onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • refried beans
  • pinto beans
  • black beans
  • cilantro
  • fat free greek yogurt, or sour cream
  • chili powder
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil on large pot over medium heat
  2. Sautee onion and bell peppers for 5 minutes
  3. Add the turkey and chili powder, breaking the meat into smaller chunks, and cook for 5 minutes or until brown
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and beans, and bring to a boil
  5. Simmer on low for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes
